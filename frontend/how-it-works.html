How it works

Up and running in minutes.

No technical setup. If you can use WhatsApp, you can run your portfolio on CribFlow.

01

Add your properties

Create each property, set its rooms and monthly rent, water rate, and a short account prefix (e.g. SRC). Takes a couple of minutes per building.

02

Onboard your tenants

Add tenants to their units with lease terms and deposit. Each unit gets a unique account number — SRC-A1 — that tenants use when they pay.

03

Let CribFlow run it

Bills generate every month. Tenants pay your paybill; payments match and receipts send automatically. You just watch collections come in — live.
