Features

Everything a landlord needs. Nothing they don't.

Purpose-built for Kenyan rentals — from paybill matching to water meters.

Billing that runs itself

Every month CribFlow generates accurate bills for each tenant — rent, water from the latest meter reading, other charges, late fees and any carried-over balance. No formulas, no double-counting, no missed months.

M-Pesa that reconciles itself

Tenants pay your own paybill using their unit account number. CribFlow matches each payment to the right tenant instantly and updates their balance — money settles directly to you, never through us.

Balances you can trust

Overpayments become credit, arrears stay visible, deposits are tracked. Every tenant's running balance is always correct — apply credit or record a refund in one tap.

Water, receipts & reminders

Log meter readings per unit and charges flow onto the bill. Professional PDF receipts and email confirmations go out automatically, and overdue reminders keep rent on time.
